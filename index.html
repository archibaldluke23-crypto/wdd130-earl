Purpose

My practice is dedicated to the exploration of intricate abstract designs through pencil work, ballpoint pen drawings, etc. I also focus on photography in various genres including landscape, portraiture, commercial, etc. I am fundamentally driven by a desire to inspire others around me to take the time to capture the beauty of the world around us, as well as the joy and beauty that comes from each and every individual. My work seeks to offer viewers a moment of reflection, as well as inspiration and joy, encouraging a deeper engagement with the often-overlooked details of the human experience.

About

The execution of my work is a meticulous journey that prioritizes slow, fine lines and repeating patterns, as well as framing, background, and other techniques in my digital photography. I primarily utilize fine-tip pencils or mechanical pencils, ballpoint pens, fine-tip pens, etc. for my traditional art pieces, and using digital photography equipment such as a DSLR camera, tripod, photoshop, etc. in my digital photography. This deliberate choice of materials is central to the narrative, often contrasting bold colors with black and white. Through my artistic lens, I aim to not simply depict, but to more fully and emotionally exhibit the complexities inherent in our personal lives as well as all-in-all in the world around us.
